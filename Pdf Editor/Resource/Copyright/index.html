CSIP

CSIP is the app designed to assist CSIP flight instructors easily create, complete, reference, and manage the different files and documents Cirrus requires for the different CSIP standardized training programs. CSIPs can fill, save, organize, manage and submit client’s training records right from the app quickly, easily and everywhere without the need for an internet connection. The app’s logic, flow and interface is designed with simplicity and speed in mind. It is intended to reduce the administrative process workload and the dependency of different systems required to complete the service delivery cycle.
